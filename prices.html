Prices

Simple Landing Page - $15 per month or $121 per year

A simple landing page is a single page with three sections. These sections are typically and about me section, details about your goods and services, and a contact section. A navigation bar will guide visitors to each section of the page. Price includes minor updates as needed, typically replacing content. If more extensive updating is required it will be quoted on a case by case basis. Some things that are not included in this price but you may want to add are a domain name if you do not already own one and a contact form. The domain you can purchase where ever you want and I will help you direct it to your website. If you would like a contact form with the information emailed directly to you there is an additional cost of $2 per month. All content is provided by you. This site is not suitable for ecommerce.
