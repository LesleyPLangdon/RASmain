Prices

Simple Landing Page - $15 per month or $121 per year

A simple landing page is a single page with three sections. These sections are typically an about me section, details about your goods and services, and a contact section. A navigation bar will guide visitors to each section of the page. Price includes minor updates as needed, typically replacing content. If more extensive updating is required it will be quoted based on a discounted rate of $25 per hour.

Some things that are not included in this price but, you may want to add are a domain name if you do not already own one and an unbranded contact form. The domain you can purchase wherever you want and I will help you direct it to your website. The MightyForms branded contact form shown on the sample sites can be included at no cost, I will help you set up your own MightyForms free account which will be your responsibility to maintain. If you want an unbranded contact form, there is an additional cost of $2 per month. All content is provided by you. This site is not suitable for ecommerce.
